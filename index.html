A few words from our side

Manohar Lal
Principal, DPS Mathura Road

We welcome you all to MUNQSMUN 2016. Initiated with a vision to be more than just a platform for school students to deliberate and negotiate on International matters. This year our students have aimed to seek perfection and present an amalgam of old and new. Keeping in mind our school's motto "Service Before Self" we hope the delegates will solve the problems plaguing our planet through global and peaceful solutions. We hope that is event will see thoughtful debating, diplomatic negotiations and bring out the leader in you. Hope to see you all at Delhi Public School, Mathura Road's MUNQSMUN 2016. All the very best.
